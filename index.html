3. Welche Daten werden verarbeitet?

Die App speichert ausschließlich Daten, die Sie selbst eingeben. Wir, der Anbieter der App, haben keinen Zugriff auf diese Daten. Die Daten werden lokal auf Ihrem Gerät gespeichert und – sofern Sie iCloud aktiviert haben – über Apples iCloud (CloudKit) zwischen Ihren Geräten synchronisiert.

  • Objektdaten: Bezeichnung, Adresse von Immobilien und Wohnungen
  • Mieterdaten: Vor- und Nachname, optional E-Mail-Adresse, Personenanzahl
  • Vertragsdaten: Mietvertragsbeginn und -ende
  • Verbrauchsdaten: Zählerstände, Zählertypen, Ablesedaten
  • Finanzdaten: Betriebs- und Heizkosten, Vorauszahlungen, Abrechnungszeiträume

2. Overview

The "Nebenkostenabrechnung" app helps landlords create utility cost statements for their tenants. We take the protection of your personal data seriously and process it confidentially in accordance with GDPR.

3. What data is processed?

The app only stores data you enter yourself. We, the provider, have no access to this data. Data is stored locally on your device and — if iCloud is enabled — synchronized via Apple iCloud (CloudKit) between your devices.

  • Property data: name and address of buildings and apartments
  • Tenant data: first and last name, optionally email address, number of persons
  • Lease data: start and end date of tenancy
  • Consumption data: meter readings, meter types, reading dates
  • Financial data: operating and heating costs, advance payments, billing periods

4. Roles under GDPR

When using the app, you as the landlord process personal data of your tenants. You are the data controller under GDPR for this data. The provider of the app (Marco Hoferer / HConsulting) has no access to your app data. Apple Inc. provides the iCloud/CloudKit storage and synchronization infrastructure and acts as a processor.

5. Legal basis

To the extent that you process personal data of your tenants in the app, this is based on:

  • Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of a contract – the lease)
  • Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR (legal obligation – §§ 556 ff. BGB)
  • Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in efficient property management)

6. Storage location and iCloud

The app stores data locally on your device using SwiftData. If you have enabled iCloud, data is additionally synchronized via Apple iCloud (CloudKit) in encrypted form. Apple servers may be located within and outside the EU. No data is transmitted to other third parties. No analytics, tracking or advertising services are used.

7. In-app purchases

The app offers an in-app purchase to unlock a billing statement. Payment is handled exclusively through the Apple App Store. Apple's Privacy Policy applies.

8. Retention period

Data remains on your device or in your iCloud until you delete it yourself in the app or uninstall the app.

9. Your rights

You have the right of access (Art. 15), rectification (Art. 16), erasure (Art. 17), restriction (Art. 18), data portability (Art. 20) and to object (Art. 21 GDPR). Because we have no access to your app data, you can exercise these rights directly in the app. For questions, contact us at m.hoferer@hconsulting.fi.
