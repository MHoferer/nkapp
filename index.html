2. Overview

This app helps landlords create utility cost statements (Nebenkostenabrechnungen) for their tenants. We take the protection of your personal data seriously and process it confidentially in accordance with GDPR.

3. What data is processed?

The app only stores data you enter yourself. We, the provider, have no access to this data. Data is stored locally on your device and — if iCloud is enabled — synchronized via Apple iCloud (CloudKit) between your devices.

Categories of data entered in the app:

  • Property data: name, address
  • Tenant data: name, optionally email, number of persons
  • Lease data: start and end date of tenancy
  • Consumption data: meter readings, reading dates
  • Financial data: operating and heating costs, advance payments

4. Roles under GDPR

When using the app, you as the landlord process personal data of your tenants. You are the data controller under GDPR. The provider of the app has no access to the content. Apple Inc. provides the iCloud/CloudKit infrastructure and acts as a processor.

5. Legal basis

Processing is based on Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of a contract), (c) (legal obligation under §§ 556 ff. BGB) and (f) (legitimate interest).

6. Storage location and iCloud

Data is stored locally using SwiftData and — if iCloud is enabled — synchronized via Apple iCloud (CloudKit) in encrypted form. Apple servers may be located within and outside the EU. No data is transmitted to other third parties. No analytics, tracking or advertising services are used.

7. In-app purchases

The app offers an in-app purchase to unlock a billing statement. Payment is handled exclusively through the Apple App Store. Apple's Privacy Policy applies.

8. Retention period

Data remains on your device or in your iCloud until you delete it yourself in the app or uninstall the app.

9. Your rights

You have the right of access (Art. 15), rectification (Art. 16), erasure (Art. 17), restriction (Art. 18), data portability (Art. 20) and to object (Art. 21 GDPR). Because we have no access to your app data, you can exercise these rights directly in the app.

10. Right to lodge a complaint
